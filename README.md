# PinTrail

Discover campus sustainability like never before. PinTrail is a mobile app that connects students and visitors to UMass Amherst's green initiatives through curated trails, interactive maps, and real-time discovery. Walk the green path and uncover solar panels, eco-gardens, and sustainability innovations hiding in plain sight.

## What is PinTrail?

PinTrail transforms how people explore and engage with sustainability at UMass Amherst. Instead of reading about environmental efforts, you experience them firsthand. The app guides you through themed sustainability trails across campus, pinpoints green initiatives on an interactive map, and nudges you with notifications when you're near something interesting. It's a self-guided tour that educates, inspires, and connects the community to real environmental impact.

## Key Features

- **Curated Sustainability Trails**: Follow themed paths that connect campus sustainability projects—from rooftop gardens to geothermal systems. Each stop tells a story of UMass's green initiatives.
- **Interactive Pin Mapping**: See every sustainability site plotted on a live campus map. Filter by category, find what's near you, and navigate with turn-by-turn guidance.
- **Smart Notifications**: Get notified when you're near an interesting sustainability site and be nudged to explore and learn.
- **Didactic Learning**: Rich context panels, infographics, and data overlays make every stop an educational moment—no lecture required.
- **Community Contributions**: Add your voice by contributing photos, reflections, and discoveries to a growing tapestry of campus sustainability stories.
- **Real Impact Tracking**: See aggregated campus sustainability stats and understand how collective action drives measurable change.
- **Responsive Mobile Design**: Optimized for outdoor use with a clean, intuitive interface designed by and for the UMass community.

## Why PinTrail?

Sustainability efforts are often invisible to students and campus visitors. PinTrail makes them discoverable. By combining location-based technology with curated storytelling, we're making environmental awareness accessible, engaging, and fun—one trail at a time.
