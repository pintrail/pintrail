Features

Discover Campus, Differently

From pin mapping to community art — PinTrail transforms how you experience sustainability at UMass.

01 📍

Curated Campus Trails

Follow themed paths that connect sustainability projects across campus — from rooftop gardens to geothermal systems. Each stop tells a story.

02 🗺️

Interactive Pin Mapping

See every green initiative plotted on a live campus map. Filter by category, find what's near you, and navigate with turn-by-turn guidance.

03 🎨

Community Collage

Add your voice. Contribute photos, reflections, and discoveries to a living, growing tapestry of campus sustainability stories.

04 📱

Didactic App Experience

Learn as you walk. Rich context panels, infographics, and data overlays make every stop an educational moment — no lecture required.

05 🖥️

UI/UX Designed for Students

Clean, intuitive interfaces designed by the UMass community, for the UMass community. Fast, accessible, and built for outdoor use.

06 🌍

Real Impact, Real Data

Track your eco-footprint, see aggregated campus sustainability stats, and understand how collective action drives measurable change.

Built by students,
for a greener future

PinTrail started at UMass Amherst's Earth Day Extravaganza - a student-led project to make campus sustainability visible, accessible, and inspiring for everyone. We believe the path to a greener campus starts with awareness.
